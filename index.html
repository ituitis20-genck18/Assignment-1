KADIKOY CAMPUS - DOGA SCHOOLS




Kadikoy campus of Doga Schools was established in 2013-2014 academic year. It teaches in kindergarten, primary school, middle school and high school groups. The campus, which is structured according to the interests, needs and expectations of the students, aims to raise students who have broad scientific horizons, participatory, self-confident cultural and technological knowledge, intellectual and able to think, by using modern education techniques.

Doga Schools teach three foreign languages in different level of classes which are:

  • English
  • German
  • Spanish
